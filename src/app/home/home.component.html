Gbenga Samuel Wemimo Ministry
International

Gbenga Samuel-Wemimo Ministry International, GSWMI, has been raised by the Holy Spirit to equip the saints as they sort out their Homo Christus (Christ-kind) status in their walk with the Holy Spirit. The Messianic Community is not ordinary, and believers are not ordinary by any standard. The ministry's mandate is to bring Neppios (Baby Christians) to the point of maturity in the things of the Spirit by the leading of the Holy Ghost. Believers will henceforth live as Christ and The Word instructs and not tossed to and fro by the elements of this world.

Pneuma School of the Supernatural and Bible College

The Pneuma School of the Supernatural Bible College, PSSBC, is a school where Believers who thirst for more of God or have come to the understanding that there is more to being a Believer than going to church. PSSBC teaches the average believer that he is not ordinary and can live the life of the supernatural.

They are taught the simplicity of the Gospel backed up by the demonstration of power. In the last 2 years, 5 classes have been launched with many individuals starting off their very own ministerial assignments
