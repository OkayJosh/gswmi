Gbenga Samuel-Wemimo Ministry International, GSWMI, has been raised by the Holy Spirit to equip the saints as they sort out their Homo Christus (Christ-kind) status in their walk with the Holy Spirit. The Messianic Community is not ordinary, and believers are not ordinary by any standard. The ministry's mandate is to bring Neppios (Baby Christians) to the point of maturity in the things of the Spirit by the leading of the Holy Ghost. Believers will henceforth live as Christ and The Word instructs and not tossed to and fro by the elements of this world.
